# Adam Hyperparameters

**Category:** training
**Difficulty:** 2
**Tags:** optimization, adam

## Question
What are the typical values for β1 and β2 in the Adam optimizer?

## Answer
**β1 = 0.9** (first moment / momentum)
**β2 = 0.999 or 0.95** (second moment / variance)

For LLM training, β2 = 0.95 is increasingly common.
