# RLHF Stages

**Category:** rlhf
**Difficulty:** 2
**Tags:** rlhf, training

## Question
What are the three stages of RLHF?

## Answer
1. **Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT)**: Train on demonstrations
2. **Reward Model Training**: Train model to predict human preferences
3. **RL Optimization (PPO/DPO)**: Optimize policy to maximize reward

Each stage builds on the previous one.
